Welcome to my personal page

I am a Ph.D student at the BCAM-Basque Center for Applied Mathematics under the supervision of Dr. Inmaculada Arostegui and Dr. Dae-Jin Lee. I belong to Applied Statistics Group research line. My thesis is focused on beta-binomial mixed models applied to health related quality of live outcomes.

I obtained my Bachelor degree in Mathematics at University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU) in June 2013. I obtained my Master's degree in Mathematical Modelling and Research, Statistics and Computing at the joint programme by University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU) , University of Zaragoza and University of Oviedo in September 2014. My master thesis was titled "Modelling of the Health Related Quality of Life by beta-binomial distribution: Application in patients with chronic diseases" supervised by Inmaculada Arostegui.

Research interests

My research interest lies in beta-binomial regression in mixed models framework, in particular in health related quality of live (HRQoL) outcomes. We are developing mixed models based on beta-binomial distribution to measure the relationship of HRQoL with patients' and disease's characteristics over time, particularly patients with chronic diseases.

I am also member of Biostit research group and member of the Spanish Biometric Society.

The main applications of my work are related to:

  • Generalized Linear Mixed Models
  • Hierarchical Generalized Linear Models
  • Beta-binomial regression
  • Health Related Quality of Life outcome data analysis
